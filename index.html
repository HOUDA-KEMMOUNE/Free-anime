Blue Lock Anime Episode 1

Show story

After reflecting on the current state of Japanese football, the Japan Football Association has decided to appoint the mysterious and eccentric coach Igo Jinbashi to fulfill their dream of winning the World Cup . Believing that Japan lacks a selfish striker with a hunger for goals, Jinbashi sets up the Blue Lock, a prison-like facility where three hundred talented strikers from high schools across Japan are isolated and compete against each other. The only survivor of the Blue Lock will earn the right to become the national team striker, and those who are defeated will be banned from joining the team forever. To join this risky venture, Isagi Yuichi is a striker who has failed to bring his high school soccer team to the national championship. That being said, after choosing to pass to his teammate who blundered instead of scoring alone, he couldn't help but wonder if the results would have been different had he been more selfish. Using this golden opportunity presented by Project Blue Look, Yoichi aims to clear his doubts and chase after his ultimate desire, to become the world's greatest striker and lead Japan to glory at the World Cup.
